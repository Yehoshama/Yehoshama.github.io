Introducing Yehoshama.com

March 2026 · 3 min read

Today, we're excited to launch Yehoshama.com — the commercial arm of the Yehoshama Foundation.

Why Yehoshama.com?

The Yehoshama Foundation has always been about building technology that empowers rather than exploits. As our open-source work grew, businesses started asking: "Can you build something like this for us?"

Yehoshama.com is our answer. We bring the same engineering standards, ethical commitment, and technical depth to commercial projects — and every dollar earned funds the Foundation's open-source mission.

What We Offer

  • Custom Software Development — From concept to deployment
  • API Design & Implementation — REST and GraphQL at scale
  • System Architecture — Infrastructure that grows with you
  • Security Audits — Finding vulnerabilities before anyone else

Our Promise

We don't sell your data. We don't cut corners. We build software that lasts — and we back it with the integrity of the Foundation.
