About Yehoshama

The commercial arm of the Yehoshama Foundation — building software that serves business with the same integrity that serves community.

Our Story

Yehoshama was born from a simple conviction: technology should empower, never exploit.

The Yehoshama Foundation began as an open-source initiative — building resilient, unexploitative software protocols and tools. As the Foundation's work gained traction, a clear need emerged: businesses wanted access to the same quality, the same principles, applied to their own challenges.

Yehoshama.com is the answer. We bring the Foundation's engineering standards, ethical commitment, and deep technical expertise to the commercial world. Every project we take on funds the Foundation's open-source mission — so when you work with us, you're also investing in technology that belongs to everyone.

We don't cut corners. We don't sell your data. We build software that lasts, for people who value substance over hype.

Our Values

Integrity

We say what we mean, deliver what we promise, and own our mistakes. Transparency isn't a marketing term — it's how we operate.

Quality

Every line of code is reviewed, every system is tested, every deployment is monitored. We don't ship until it's right.

No Exploitation

We will never sell your data, mine your usage patterns, or lock you into a system designed to extract value from you. Your software belongs to you.

Transparency

Open communication throughout every project. You'll always know where things stand, what's working, and what needs attention.
